Zen Stack – Privacy Policy

Effective date:

Zen Stack is an Android casual game by PHI 1123 Apps (“we,” “us,” or “our”). This policy explains what data the app and its third-party services may collect and how it’s used.

1) What we collect

  • Crash & diagnostics (Firebase Crashlytics) – device model, OS version, app version, stack traces, and crash context. Used solely to diagnose stability issues.
  • Ads (Google AdMob) – AdMob (and its partners) may collect advertising identifiers and usage data to serve and measure ads. You can reset or limit ad personalization in Android settings.
  • App usage (Firebase Analytics, minimal) – basic, aggregated event data (e.g., session starts) to understand overall engagement. We do not collect names, email, or contact info in-app.

2) How we use the data

  • Improve stability, performance, and user experience (Crashlytics/Analytics).
  • Serve and measure in-app advertising (AdMob).

3) Third-party services

  • Google AdMob – see Google’s privacy & partner policies for advertising and ad measurement.
  • Firebase Crashlytics – used for crash reporting and diagnostics.

4) Data retention

Diagnostics and analytics are retained per the third-party providers’ standard policies. We do not keep personal identifiers on our own servers.

5) Children

Zen Stack is intended for users aged 13+. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6) Your choices

  • Android settings → Google → Ads: reset advertising ID and limit ad personalization.
  • Disable analytics/crash reporting at the OS level if available.

7) Changes

We may update this policy. We will revise the “Effective date” above and publish changes here.
